# 📧 Teacher Announcement Email — Draft

Use the template below to inform teachers about the new Workshop Registration System.  
Fill in the `[placeholders]` with your school's information before sending.

---

**To:** All Teachers — [School Name]

**Subject:** New Workshop Registration System – Register Your Students

---

Dear [Teacher Name],

I hope this message finds you well.

I am pleased to introduce a new **Workshop Registration System** that I have developed to streamline the process of signing students up for our upcoming workshops.

### How It Works

1. **Register a student** – Visit the registration portal at [Workshop Link] and fill out the form with your name, email, and the desired workshop.
2. **One workshop at a time** – Each registration reserves a seat for one student in one workshop.
3. **Need to make a change?** – If you need to switch a student to a different workshop, simply re-submit the form using the **same email address**. The system will automatically update the workshop selection and send you a confirmation email regarding the modification.

No need to contact the administration for changes — the system handles updates seamlessly.

### Why Use This System?

- **Fast & easy** – Register in under a minute.
- **Automatic confirmations** – You will receive an email confirmation for every registration and every update.
- **Real-time availability** – The system shows which workshops have open seats so you can make informed choices.

I encourage all teachers to begin using the system for their students. Your feedback is always welcome as we continue to improve our tools.

Thank you for your cooperation and support.

Best regards,

[Your Full Name]  
[Your Title / Position]  
[School Name]
